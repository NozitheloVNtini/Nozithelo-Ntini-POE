## Project Overview

Chef Nozy is a small food company established in 2022 that specialises in providing personalised food service to small businesses, personal events and special occasions. The brand plans to offer a more personalised and unique alternative to conventional catering services.

The company focuses on quality of foods, details and customised menus. Services are served as private dining, events catering and menu planning.

Nowadays, Chef Nozy works mostly using social media like Instagram and WhatsApp. This initiative is associated with the creation of a professional site to enhance the online presence of the brand, highlight services and enable clients to make booking requests.

## Website Goals and Objectives

### Key Goals

* Increase online visibility of the Chef Nozy brand
* Provide clear and structured information about services and menus
* Enable potential clients to submit booking enquiries
* Showcase previous work and food presentation
* Build credibility and professionalism

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Monthly website visitors
* Number of enquiry form submissions
* Number of confirmed bookings


## Key Features and Functionality

### Homepage

* Brand introduction
* Overview of services
* Featured food images

### About Page

* Background of Chef Nozy
* Culinary philosophy

### Services Page

* Private dining experiences

### Menu / Gallery Page
