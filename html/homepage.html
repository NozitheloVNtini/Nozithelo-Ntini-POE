What's Chef Nozy's Vibe?

Private chef experiences and curated event catering in Johannesburg.

I design unique dining experiences for our clients, be it a private dinner, small celebration or event tailored to their specific requirements. I carefully prepare every dish from fresh ingredients and consider flavour and presentation.

The end goal is to bring people together through great food and experiences with everything from custom menus to bespoke service.


What I Do

I offer various catering services that can cater to a range of clients and events:

Private Chef Experience


Have a restaurant quality experience in the comfort of your home! Great for date nights, small parties, or special events. Every experience is well thought out to be in line with the mood and style of an event, including what to put on the menu and how to serve.

Example experiences:

  • Romantic dinner for two with a three course plated menu
  • Small birthday gathering with shared style dishes
  • Private home dining with a curated multi course experience

Event Catering


You might consider my catering services for small birthday, celebration, private event, functions. The menus are customised according to the theme and requirements of the event.

Example events:

  • Birthday celebrations with finger food and mains
  • Private family gatherings with shared platters

Personalised Menu Arrangement


Collaborate with me to design a menu that suits your tastes, allergies and theme of your event.

Example focus areas:

  • Custom fine dining style menu creation
  • Dietary specific menus (vegetarian, allergy friendly, etc)
  • Themed event menus based on mood or occasion

Meal Prep Services

Convenient meal prep options for individuals or families looking for high quality and ready to be eaten meals.


All services are flexible and of the highest standard.

Perfect for:

  • Weekly meals for busy professionals
  • Balanced home style meals for families
  • Health focused meal planning for consistency
